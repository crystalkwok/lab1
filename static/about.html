Hello there! My name is Crystal Kwok. I'm currently a Cognitive Psychology major, Computer Science minor at the University of California, San Diego. I stumbled upon the world of HCI/UX and instantly fell in love, so I'm currently taking more HCI classes along with Cognitive Psychology, Social Psychology, and Computer Science classes.

I'm a left-brained artist and a technologically-adverse coder, but it makes for someone passionate about user-centered design! I fell in love with UX design because it combines all my passions-- people, research, artistic design, and coding. I firmly believe that every design should emotionally engage each person by contributing to making what is most important to the user matter most. What I love most about UX design is that it is revolutionary, because designs must constantly change to fit the different needs in different contexts in our ever changing world, and design can change how people think. UX design is designing to solve problems for change, which calls for new innovative and creative ideas. My education and research experience in Psychology helps me, when designing, focus on the users and understand the motivations that drive them.

When I am not working on my HCI projects, I am crafting, dancing, painting, cooking and baking, watching Masterchef Australia, obsessing over typography, and serving with my church. :)
