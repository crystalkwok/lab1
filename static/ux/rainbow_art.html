what do you do here?



My main roles at Rainbow Art & Rainbow Canvas are being an assistant art instructor to children and adults, which I have started since June 2010, and being the UX designer. Rainbow Art is a local art school for children, and Rainbow Canvas offers various single classes where an art instructor will instruct step-by-step on the featured artwork of the night (similar to Wine & Canvas, but without the wine). I have taught colored pencil, watercolor, markers, and acrylic painting to various students.


I recently became the UX designer of Rainbow Art & Rainbow Canvas simply because I asked for more roles in practicing my Adobe Creative Suite skills, but I am also dedicated to making the experience at Rainbow Art and Rainbow Canvas more user-centered and to solve problems presented by the users.


I have made flyers, designed the Rainbow Canvas logo, started coding the company website, and made sample paintings for Rainbow Canvas.
